Homemade Mac and Cheese

This classic baked mac and cheese recipe with a buttered bread crumb topping is creamy and

comforting. It's easy to make the cheese sauce from scratch on your stovetop, starting with

a roux and adding milk, Cheddar, and Parmesan, resulting in a rich, decadent sauce that

coats every nook and cranny of the noodles. Serve this comfort-food macaroni and cheese

casserole with grilled meats, sloppy Joes, fried chicken or alongside a salad for a meatless dinner.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time:45 mins

Total Time:55 mins

Servings:4

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour/li>
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread crumbs

Directions

  1. Boil the macaroni in salted water until the noodles are al dente. Drain and

    transfer to a prepared baking dish

  2. Melt butter, then whisk in the flour. Whisk in the milk, bring to a simmer,

    and stir in the cheeses. Season with salt and pepper and continue simmering

    until the sauce is thick. Pour the sauce over the noodles and stir.

  3. Melt two tablespoons of butter in a skillet, add the bread crumbs, and and

    cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.toast until the crumbs are brown. Spread

    the topping over the macaroni

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the topping is golden brown.
